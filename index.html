This Year's Themes

Under our overarching theme "From Ideas to Impact: The New Wave of Change Makers," we have two exciting sub-themes for submissions:

Solving Global Problems with Innovative Ideas

This theme encourages students to explore how creative and impactful ideas can be used to tackle some of the world's most pressing issues. Your article should:

  • Link to a UN Sustainable Development Goal
  • Propose an innovative solution
  • Explain potential impact
  • Consider implementation challenges
View Rubric

Fun and Creative Ideas

This theme focuses on light-hearted, imaginative, and out-of-the-box ideas. Your article can explore:

  • New ways to engage in sports or activities
  • Creative social initiatives
  • Unusual innovations
  • Entertainment with impact
